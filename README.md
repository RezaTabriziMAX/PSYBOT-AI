# SPCFRAG

## Discover SOL Fragments in Orbit

SPCFRAG is a lightweight on-chain experiment that transforms gameplay into measurable blockchain value.

Players control a small astronaut unit navigating through orbital space filled with drifting debris, abandoned satellites, and fragments left behind by decades of human activity.

The mission is simple:

Clean the orbit.  
Collect fragments.  
Convert them into on-chain value.

Every piece of debris recovered becomes a **Space Fragment**, the core resource of the SPCFRAG ecosystem. These fragments represent player participation and can later be converted into tokens or rewards within the Solana ecosystem.

Rather than functioning purely as a traditional game or a speculative token, SPCFRAG is designed as an experimental system connecting gameplay mechanics, player attention, and decentralized liquidity.

Every fragment collected represents activity.  
Every conversion represents economic output.  
Together they form a fragment-powered ecosystem driven by the community.

SPCFRAG explores a simple idea:

Can gameplay, attention, and blockchain incentives combine to create a lightweight on-chain economy?
