## Overview

SPCFRAG is a lightweight on-chain experiment that explores how simple gameplay can be transformed into real digital value.

In this system, players control a small astronaut unit navigating through orbital space filled with drifting debris, broken satellite components, and fragments left behind by decades of human activity. The objective is simple: clean the orbit by collecting these scattered objects.

Every piece of debris recovered is converted into a resource called **Space Fragments**, which forms the core of the project's economy. These fragments represent the transformation of gameplay activity into digital energy. As fragments accumulate, they can be exchanged for the project’s token or redeemed through reward pools connected to the Solana ecosystem.

Rather than functioning purely as a traditional game or a speculative token, SPCFRAG is designed as a hybrid experiment connecting gameplay mechanics with a fragment-based on-chain economy. Each fragment collected represents player participation, each conversion represents economic activity, and the growing pool of fragments reflects the collective effort of the community working together to clean the orbit.

Through this simple but dynamic structure, SPCFRAG explores a fundamental idea: whether gameplay, attention, and decentralized liquidity can combine to form a small but sustainable on-chain ecosystem.

## Core Concept

SPCFRAG operates through a simple gameplay and economic loop:

Play → Collect Debris → Generate Fragments → Convert to Value

Players interact with the environment, collect debris, and generate fragments which can later be converted into tokens or rewards. This system transforms player attention and activity into measurable blockchain-based output.

---

## Gameplay Flow

### 1. Enter Orbit

Players enter the orbital environment and control an astronaut unit navigating through space filled with floating debris, abandoned satellite components, and scattered fragments.

### 2. Collect Debris

As players recover space junk and broken equipment, the system converts these objects into **Space Fragments**. Different types of debris produce different fragment values depending on size and rarity.

### 3. Convert Fragments

Fragments can be accumulated and converted into project tokens or redeemed through reward pools connected to the Solana network.

### 4. Power the Ecosystem

As more players participate, fragments continuously circulate through the system. Player activity generates fragments, fragments create rewards, and rewards attract more participation, forming a community-driven economic loop.

## Fragment Economy

The SPCFRAG ecosystem revolves around a core resource called **Space Fragments**.

Fragments represent:

- Player participation  
- Gameplay effort  
- Digital energy generated through activity  

Fragments can be used to:

- Convert into project tokens  
- Access reward pools  
- Participate in future ecosystem features  

This fragment-based economy connects gameplay interaction directly to on-chain value.
